About Me

My name is Chris. I have a passion for the creative.

I love art, and being able to make my art interactive. I started with game devopment as a child through visual coding, using programs such as Construct, Multimedia Fusion 2, and RPG Maker. Focusing on creating assets, and learning design.

I discovered Unity 3D and quickly started learning C#, falling in love with the techical side of development. I spend my free time learning the language, design patterns, and the value of readable maintainable code.

I am still growing as a developer! Now focusing on learning full-stack development. I have coded, written, and designed this website to be a portfolio for myself.
